Limitations of BMI

Gender

The development and body fat composition of girls and boys vary with age. Consequently, a child's age and gender are considered when evaluating their BMI.

Age

In aging individuals, increased body fat and muscle loss may cause BMI to underestimate body fat content.

Muscle

BMI may misclassify muscular individuals as overweight or obese, as it doesn't differentiate muscle from fat.

Pregnancy

Expectant mothers experience weight gain due to their growing baby. Maintaining a healthy pre-pregnancy BMI is advisable to minimize health risks for both mother and child.

Race

Certain health concerns may affect individuals of some Black and Asian origins at lower BMIs than others. To learn more, it is advise to discuss this with your GP practice nurse.
